Penne Gorgonzola

description:

Penne Gorgonzola is a pasta dish consisting of Penne pasta. It was one of the many courses served to Luffy during the farewell banquet concluding his first visit to the island.

Ingredients:

  • 180g penne
  • 2 slices ham
  • Italian parsley
  • dash pepper
  • 2-3 tbsp powdered parmesan cheese
  • 50g blue cheese
  • 100mL heavy cream
  • 100mL milk
